What is TillCoin ($TCO)?

TillCoin $TCO is my own utility web3 token on the Polygon network. With this token, you can invest in "me" while being cool among other people and accessing benefits/premium subscribtions in all of my projects. For lifetime as long as you own TCO.

$TCO can be directly traded for $MATIC on the Polygon network back and forth, and it has a dynamic price because of its own market pool. The more people invest MATIC in TillCoin, the more your tokens will be worth when you sell them. How does it work and how can you support me with it? Think of it as a MATIC reserve. When you send MATIC to the TillCoin smart contract, you get a TCO token back. When you own one or some of these tokens, you get access to a lot of members' stuff, like free premium on all software that only I produce, and much more. When many people buys TCO and its supply grows, the MATIC reserve will hold more assets, and you can redeem your MATIC tokens + profit.
